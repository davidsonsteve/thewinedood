Fox Island Blackberry

The Dood's most personal wine, every blackberry hand-picked by the Dood and his family.

These berries come from the sunny southeast point of the Island. (Known to the party types of the 60's and 70's as Kegger's Point.) It takes a pound of berries to make a single bottle!

Unlike most commercial fruit wines, the Dood's blackberry is presented at absolute dryness. It is the flavor of berries in early summer; light, tart and fresh.

With no added tannins, and low sulfites, this deep red-purple wine drinks like a white. The nose is soft and delicate, and is best enjoyed lightly chilled.


2014 2015

Making notes
